AgroConnect The Problem & Opportunity

A broken chain — at every link.

Ghana's agriculture feeds 33 million people and earns billions in exports. Yet the system that supports it is fragmented, analogue, and deeply inefficient.

  • Farmers sell at farm-gate prices 60–80% below market — middlemen capture the margin
  • 40% of harvests rot before reaching consumers or export markets
  • Farmers have zero access to formal credit — no collateral, no credit history, no path to growth
  • International buyers wanting verified Ghanaian supply have no trusted platform to source from
  • Cross-border payments are slow, risky, and captured by brokers — no escrow, no protection

The opportunity is enormous.

Infrastructure, policy, and global demand have aligned. The window is open.

$10B+ Ghana agricultural GDP
$13B+ Ghana total exports annually
$3.4T AfCFTA continental market

Only 3% of agricultural trade in Ghana happens through digital platforms. Ghana is one of the world's top producers of cocoa, shea, cashew, pineapple, and fresh fish — yet most of it is sold untraced, through middlemen, at a fraction of its international value. AgroConnect is the platform that changes this.
